On Monday 18 May 2020 the current players in Zig-Zag GS and Zig-Zag GT will be reorganised into two new teams. One of the teams will assemble in the current Zig-Zag GT team, this will be a competitive team looking to move up the ranks. The other will assemble in Zig-Zag GB. Zig-Zag GB will be a new team with zero cups to give players easy wins and big rewards.

The page identifies which team each player has been assigned to.
The destinations for current Zig-Zag GS Players can be found here.
The destinations for current Zig-Zag GT Players can be found here

Players will have until 15:30 pm (UK time) on 18 May to move to their new teams before matchmaking will commence.

Once the two teams are set-up there will be movement between them depending on performance. The top players from Zig-Zag GB may move to GT if they score well, the bottom players from GT will be asked to move to GB.
