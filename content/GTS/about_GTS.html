Zig-Zag GTS is the second team in the Zig-Zag family. It was formed in May 2020 (during the Timesplitting event) as a combination of the top players in Zig-Zag GS (former ZZ second team) and Zig-Zag GT (former ZZ academy team).

GTS was last recorded as having 9637 cups and being ranked 129 in the world. This measurement was taken going into the match against Rally Estonia (summer thrills & spills 3).

Since formation GTS has played 11 matches, winning 8 of those (a win percentage of 73%). Of their most recent 11 matches GT have won 8 (a recent win percentage of 73%).
