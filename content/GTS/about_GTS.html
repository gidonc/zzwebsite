Zig-Zag GT is the third team in the Zig-Zag family. It was formed at the end of December 2019 (during the Let it Slide event) as an academy team for Zig-Zag. It was known as Zig-Zag Racerz. It changed its name to Zig-Zag GT after merging with parts of two other teams: first, the leadership of GrandTour (formerly Khaleesi) and second, parts of GreasySpanner.

GT was last recorded as having 8549 cups and being ranked 803 in the world. This measurement was taken going into the match against BvB09 Racing (timesplitting 2).

Since formation GT has played 69 matches, winning 62 of those (a win percentage of 90%). Of their most recent 15 matches GT have won 13 (a recent win percentage of 87%).
