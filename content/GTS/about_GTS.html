GTS was last recorded as having 10250 cups and being ranked 298 in the world. This measurement was taken going into the match against InFusionRBLUE (suspension check 3).

Since formation GTS has played 31 matches, winning 20 of those (a win percentage of 65%). Of their most recent 15 matches GT have won 8 (a recent win percentage of 53%).
