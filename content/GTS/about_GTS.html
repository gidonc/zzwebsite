Zig-Zag GTS is the second team in the Zig-Zag family. It was formed in May 2020 (during the Timesplitting event) as a combination of the top players in Zig-Zag GS (former ZZ second team) and Zig-Zag GT (former ZZ academy team).

GTS was last recorded as having 8987 cups and being ranked 372 in the world. This measurement was taken going into the match against ch’ti racing (high gear low gear 2).

Since formation GTS has played 3 matches, winning 3 of those (a win percentage of 100%). Of their most recent 3 matches GT have won 3 (a recent win percentage of 100%).
