Zig-Zag RZR is the first team in the Zig-Zag family. RZR was formed (as Zig-Zag Racers) at the end of June 2019 (at the start of the Newfangled vehicles event). It was born from the embers of rizzlaracers, a some time top 30 team brought down by an inactive leader.

RZR was last recorded as having 10653 cups and being ranked 47 in the world. This measurement was taken going into the match against SQUADRON 303 (don’t choke 1).

Since formation RZR has played 164 matches, winning 120 of those (a win percentage of 73%). Of their most recent 15 matches RZR have won 10 (a recent win percentage of 67%).
