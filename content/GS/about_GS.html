Zig-Zag GS is the second team in the Zig-Zag family. GS was known as GreasySpanner before joining the Zig-Zag family. GreasySpanner joined the Zig-Zag Family in Febuary 2020 (during the Bill My Valentine event).

GS currently has 8456 cups and is ranked 388 in the world. This was measured going into the match against Persian Race (canyonball 2).

Since recording began when joining Zig-Zag, GS has played 38 matches, winning 21 of those (a win percentage of 55%). Of their most recent 15 matches GS have won 8 (a recent win percentage of 53%).
