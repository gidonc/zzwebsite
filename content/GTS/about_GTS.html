Zig-Zag GTS is the second team in the Zig-Zag family. It was formed in May 2020 (during the Timesplitting event) as a combination of the top players in Zig-Zag GS (former ZZ second team) and Zig-Zag GT (former ZZ academy team).

GTS was last recorded as having 10790 cups and being ranked 371 in the world. This measurement was taken going into the match against El Salvador SV (breathe in, breathe out 4).

Since formation GTS has played 54 matches, winning 33 of those (a win percentage of 61%). Of their most recent 15 matches GT have won 9 (a recent win percentage of 60%).
