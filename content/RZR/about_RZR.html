Zig-Zag RZR is the first team in the Zig-Zag family. RZR was formed (as Zig-Zag Racers) at the end of June 2019 (at the start of the Newfangled vehicles event). It was born from the embers of rizzlaracers, a some time top 30 team brought down by an inactive leader.

RZR was last recorded as having 10369 cups and being ranked 47 in the world. This measurement was taken going into the match against Team China (sweaty palms 2).

Since formation RZR has played 155 matches, winning 115 of those (a win percentage of 74%). Of their most recent 15 matches RZR have won 11 (a recent win percentage of 73%).
